# 💬 TomodachiShare PC

Welcome to the simple and reliable PC utility for setting up and managing your TomodachiShare experience! If you are looking for a smooth and straightforward tool to handle your configuration, you are in the right place. Our application is designed to be lightweight, fast, and incredibly easy to use.

🚀 **Features**
* 🎨 **Custom Setup:** Easily customize your preferences and configurations.
* ⚙️ **Quick Access:** Launch your setup instantly without complex configurations.
* 🛡️ **Safe & Stable:** Built with stability and performance in mind.
* 📥 **Simple Setup:** No extra software or complex dependencies required.

---

## 📥 Installation

Getting started takes only a few seconds:

1. Navigate to the **[Releases](../../releases/tag/Release)** section of the repository.
2. Download the `Installer.exe` file.
3. Run the installer and follow the simple on-screen instructions.

That’s it! The application is ready to use right after installation.

---

## 💡 How to Use

1. Launch the utility from your desktop or Start menu.
2. Select your preferred options and settings in the main dashboard.
3. Start sharing and enjoy the experience!

---

## 🛠️ Requirements

* **Operating System:** Windows 10 or Windows 11.
* **Storage:** Less than 100 MB of free disk space.
* **Network:** Internet connection required only for downloading updates and resources.
